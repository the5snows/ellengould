Ellen Gould

Music for All Occasions

 

Ellen Gould is a professional musician and entertainer based out of Northern Kentucky specializing in live music for any occasion. With over 30 years of experience, she has toured and performed extensively both across the United States and overseas and in venues ranging from bars and private events to the ballroom at the Grand Ole Opry.

Ellen's repertoire spans a wide array of genres and includes everything from pop hits to traditional country and folk standards, as well as a large selection of classical pieces. In addition to being an accomplished singer, Ellen is a talented instrumentalist capable of performing on guitar, piano, banjo, and fiddle.
